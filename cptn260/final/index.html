About

J.R. Muri is a former chemical technologist turned photographer and inquisitive student of web development. His developer journey began in 2018, with enrollment at Mount Royal University for a web developer certification, during which he studied the fundamentals of web design, responsive layout, and accessibility. Advancing into semantic HTML5, CSS3, Javascript, MySQL, and PHP to completion. The experience left him feeling nauseated and inspired.

Festivals

Wicked Woods

A smaller festival located out by the fairmont hotsprings. The festival grounds overlook a wetland and though it is a smaller event still, it's growing quickly.

Circle Carnival

A local Calgary event, always held within the city. It's a collaboration of YYC Food Trucks, BassBus, and Village Brewery. They aim to have the location change each year, in order to raise awareness of communities in the city.
